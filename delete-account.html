Account Deletion - Amot

Last Updated: February 16, 2026

Request Account Deletion

You can request the deletion of your Amot account and all associated data at any time. Once your account is deleted, this action cannot be undone.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to jack.ladrera@gmail.com from the email address associated with your Amot account.
  2. Use the subject line: "Amot Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include your full name and registered email address in the body of the email.
  4. We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Your account information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Your profile information (display name, profile picture)
  • Your payment method preferences
  • Your friend connections
  • Your group memberships
  • Your push notification tokens and device information
  • Your activity history and poke notifications

What Data May Be Retained

Certain data may be retained for a limited period after account deletion for the following reasons:

  • Bill and payment records: Records of bills you participated in may be retained for up to 90 days for other participants' reference and dispute resolution purposes. Your personal identifiers will be anonymized in these records.
  • Legal obligations: Data required to comply with legal or regulatory obligations may be retained as required by law.

Additional Retention Period

After your deletion request is processed, all personal data will be permanently removed within 90 days. Anonymized or aggregated data that cannot be used to identify you may be retained indefinitely.
