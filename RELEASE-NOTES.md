# Amot - Release Notes

## Version 1.0.3 - UX Improvements

**Release Date:** March 2026

### What's New

#### Activity Feed
- **Deep Linking** - Tap any activity item to go directly to the related bill or group, no more manual searching

#### Payments
- **Mark as Received** - Bill payers can now proactively mark a payment as received (e.g. cash paid on the spot) without waiting for the other person to mark it first

## Version 1.0.0 - Initial Release

**Release Date:** January 2026

We're excited to introduce Amot, your friendly bill splitting companion for groups and friends!

### Key Features

#### Bill Management
- **Create and Split Bills** - Easily create bills and split them among friends or group members
- **Multiple Split Options** - Split equally or customize amounts for each person
- **Bill Categories** - Organize bills with custom icons and categories
- **Payment Tracking** - Keep track of who owes what and who has paid

#### Groups & Friends
- **Create Groups** - Set up groups for roommates, trips, events, or any shared expenses
- **Manage Friends** - Add friends and easily split bills with your connections
- **Group Activities** - View all group transactions and activities in one place

#### Payments
- **Payment Integration** - Support for GCash and PayMaya payment methods
- **Payment Status** - Real-time updates on payment status
- **Payment Notifications** - Get notified when payments are made or requested

#### Social Features
- **Poke Feature** - Send friendly reminders to friends about pending payments
- **Activity Feed** - Stay updated with all your bill splitting activities
- **Profile Management** - Customize your profile and manage your account

#### Notifications
- **Push Notifications** - Receive alerts for new bills, payment requests, and activities
- **Smart Alerts** - Stay informed without being overwhelmed

#### Fun & Games
- **Slot Game** - Fun mini-game feature to decide who pays the bill

### Technical Improvements
- Built with latest Expo SDK 54
- Target Android API Level 35 for latest security and performance
- Optimized navigation experience
- Enhanced notification system
- Improved design and user interface
- Better error handling and stability

### What's Next?
We're committed to making Amot even better! Stay tuned for future updates with new features and improvements.
